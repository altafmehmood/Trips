üí° Edinburgh Walking Tips

  • Weather: January is cold (3-7¬∞C) and often windy - layer up and bring waterproofs
  • Daylight: Sunset ~4:00 PM - start early for hill walks
  • Footwear: Hills can be muddy and slippery - waterproof boots recommended
  • Mix & Match: Combine morning hill walk (Arthur's Seat) with afternoon urban walk (Royal Mile)
