‚ö†Ô∏è Winter Hiking Safety & Flexibility

  • Check forecast daily: Be prepared to swap trails based on conditions
  • High winds (50+ km/h): Avoid exposed ridges (Catbells, Skiddaw) ‚Üí Choose sheltered valleys (Aira Force, Easedale Tarn)
  • Rain/wet conditions: Waterfalls are spectacular (Aira Force) ‚Üí Avoid muddy/slippery ridges
