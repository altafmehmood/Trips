‚õ∞Ô∏è Hill Walks Near Glasgow (30-60 min drive)

üèûÔ∏è Campsie Glen

Distance: 2.5 km (1.5 mi)
Duration: 1.5 hours

Difficulty: Easy-Moderate

‚õ∞Ô∏è Conic Hill (Loch Lomond)

Difficulty: Moderate

Features: Panoramic Loch Lomond views, islands, wooded ascent
From Glasgow: 45-min drive to Balmaha
Terrain: Easy gradient through trees, steep after, wee scramble at top

üèîÔ∏è Campsie Fells Circular

From Glasgow: 30-min drive north
Terrain: Boggy areas, steep descents, serious hill walking
Best for: Experienced hikers, full day, stunning views

‚õ∞Ô∏è Ben Lomond (Munro)

üí° Glasgow Walking & Hiking Tips

  • Morning of Day 6: Best time for walks before driving to Edinburgh (early start recommended)
  • Quick city walks: Kelvingrove Park (1 hour) perfect for morning before checkout
  • Half-day options: Campsie Glen (3 hrs total including drive) or Conic Hill (5 hrs total)

  • Time management: Factor in drive to Edinburgh (1.5 hrs) - don't overcommit on Day 6 morning
