Day 5 - Tuesday, April 28
Paklenica National Park & Drive to Split

üöó Drive to Paklenica

  • 45 minutes from Zadar (47km)
  • Part of Velebit Mountain range
  • Croatia's premier climbing destination

‚õ∞Ô∏è About Paklenica National Park

  • Dramatic karst landscape with vertical cliffs up to 400m
  • Two impressive canyons: Velika (Big) and Mala (Small) Paklenica
  • Over 400 climbing routes - rock climbing paradise
  • Diverse wildlife: bears, wolves, lynx, eagles (rarely seen)
  • Films including Winnetou Westerns filmed here in 1960s

ü•æ Hiking Options

Velika Paklenica Canyon Trail (Recommended)

  • 2-4 hours round trip depending on how far you go
  • Follow canyon floor along Velika Paklenica stream
  • Towering cliffs on both sides - spectacular views
  • Watch rock climbers on famous routes
  • Can hike to Paklenica Mountain Hut (2 hours, 400m ascent)

Manita Peƒá Cave (Optional Extension)

  • Add 30 minutes from main trail
  • 450m total ascent from park entrance
  • Cave with impressive stalactites and stalagmites
  • Guided tour required (check opening times)

üì∏ What to Expect

  • Rugged mountain scenery - complete contrast from coastal areas
  • Clear mountain stream flowing through canyon
  • Limestone cliffs popular with climbers from around world
  • Well-marked trails with moderate difficulty
  • Spring wildflowers in April

üöó Drive to Split

  • 2.5 hours from Paklenica (165km)
  • Scenic coastal route along Adriatic
  • Pass through traditional Dalmatian villages
  • Arrive Split late afternoon/evening

Local Insights

Zadar Tips

  • Visit Sea Organ during day AND at sunset - completely different experiences
  • Old Town is small - can see everything in half day
  • Free parking can be challenging - arrive early or use paid lots
  • Best gelato at Slastiƒçarnica kod Mije

Paklenica Tips

  • Bring hiking boots - rocky terrain
  • Water and snacks - small cafe at entrance only
  • Start hike early to have time for Split drive
  • Park entrance fee required
  • Trail can be busy in peak season but April should be quieter

Driving Tips

  • Croatian coastal road (Jadranska magistrala) is scenic but winding
  • Allow extra time - views will make you want to stop
  • Many tunnels on route - headlights required
  • GPS recommended for navigation

What to Pack for This Section

  • Layers - coastal breeze in Zadar, mountain air at Paklenica
  • Hiking boots for Paklenica
  • Comfortable walking shoes for Zadar Old Town
  • Swimsuit (if weather warm enough for coastal dip)
