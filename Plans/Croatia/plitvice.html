üèûÔ∏è About Plitvice Lakes

  • 16 interconnected lakes cascading through limestone canyons
  • Stunning turquoise and emerald colors from minerals and organisms
  • Constantly evolving - travertine barriers create new formations
  • Ancient forests with diverse wildlife (bears, wolves, lynx - rarely seen)
  • Croatia's oldest and largest national park

Trail Options (choose based on time and fitness):

Trail C (Recommended) - 4-6 hours, 8km, Moderate

  • Extended route covering both upper and lower lakes
  • Includes boat ride across Lake Kozjak
  • Best balance of highlights and time

Trail K (For Adventurous) - 6-8 hours, 22km, Difficult

  • Longest and most comprehensive trail
  • See entire park with less crowds on upper sections
  • Start very early if attempting this one

Trail A or B - 2-4 hours, 3.5-4km, Easy

  • Shorter routes focusing on lower lakes
  • Good for limited time or easier pace
  • Still see major highlights

üí° Plitvice Tips
Essential Information

What to Bring

  • Good hiking shoes - Wooden boardwalks can be slippery when wet
  • Water and snacks - Limited food options inside park
  • Light rain jacket - Waterfall spray and April showers possible
  • Camera - One of most photogenic places in Croatia
  • Sunscreen and hat

Park Rules

  • Stay on marked wooden boardwalks and paths
  • No swimming allowed (protecting ecosystem)
  • No picnicking inside park
  • Pack out all trash

Entrance Info

  • Two main entrances: Entrance 1 (Lower Lakes) and Entrance 2 (Upper Lakes)
  • Buy tickets online in advance to save time
  • Ticket includes panoramic train, electric boat, and hiking
  • Arrive early (8-9 AM) to beat tour groups

Photography Tips

  • Morning light best for turquoise colors
  • Polarizing filter helps with water reflections
  • Long exposure for waterfall silky effect (bring tripod)
  • Boardwalks make unique foreground elements

Dining

  • Restaurants at park entrances (decent but touristy)
  • Better dining in nearby villages (Rastoke village charming option)
  • If staying overnight, hotels have restaurants
