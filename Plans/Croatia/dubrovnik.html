Local Insights

City Walls Tips

  • Buy tickets in advance online to skip queues
  • Go counterclockwise (right from Pile entrance) - stairs easier this direction
  • Wear comfortable shoes - marble can be slippery
  • Start at 8 AM for best light and fewer people

Getting Around

  • Old Town is completely pedestrian-only
  • Hotels in Lapad (~2km away) cheaper than Old Town
  • Local buses frequent and cheap to Old Town
  • Walking everywhere in Old Town is easiest

Where to Stay

  • Old Town: Most atmospheric but expensive, can be noisy
  • Lapad: Beach area, good value, 15 min bus to Old Town
  • Gru≈æ: Port area, budget-friendly, less touristy
