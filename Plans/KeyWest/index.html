‚ö†Ô∏è Book Ferry in Advance

Yankee Freedom III is the only ferry to Dry Tortugas. Book 60-90 days ahead at drytortugas.com. Cost $215-235/person includes breakfast, lunch, snorkel gear, and fort tour.

üåßÔ∏è Weather Backup Plan

Ferry cancels for rough weather. If Dry Tortugas is cancelled, here's the backup day:

  • Morning: Fort Zachary Taylor beach + snorkeling from shore
  • Late Morning: Ernest Hemingway Home & Museum (30-45 min)
  • Lunch: El Siboney (authentic Cuban, locals' favorite)
  • Afternoon: Key West Butterfly Conservatory or kayak mangrove tour
  • Sunset: Sunset sail with Sebago or Fury Catamaran

üíä Seasickness Warning

The 2.5-hour Gulf crossing can be rough. Take Dramamine/Bonine the night before AND morning of. Sit outside on the upper deck mid-ship. Don't read or look at phones during crossing.

  • Marathon Beach Resort - Isla Bella, Tranquility Bay, or Hawks Cay

üéí Packing Essentials

  • Reef-safe sunscreen - SPF 50 (Keys sun is intense)
  • Dramamine/Bonine - For Dry Tortugas ferry
  • Snorkel mask - Optional, provided on tours
  • Water shoes - For rocky beach entries
  • Light jacket - Cold fronts can drop temps to 60s

üí° Pro Tips

  • Gas up in Marathon and Key West (limited stations between)
  • Bahia Honda fills early on weekends - arrive by 10 AM
  • Key West parking is $4-5/hr - use hotel parking and walk
  • Cash for El Siboney (Cuban restaurant, cash only)
  • Bring water bottle - Free refills on Dry Tortugas ferry
